The challenge

Great boutiques run on precision.
Most rosters are still made on instinct.

VIC visits uncovered

A VIC client arrives and their preferred advisor is on a different shift. The moment — and the relationship — is lost.

Skill and role gaps

No floor manager on closing. Cashier understaffed on a Saturday. Issues you only discover mid-shift.

Language mismatches

Luxury clients expect to be served in their preferred language. A roster built without that data can't guarantee it.

The solution

Every shift earns a score.
Five criteria, one number.
